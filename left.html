About

Robert "Porter" Blakeley is an electrical engineer (student) and electronics technician with experience spanning avionics, embedded systems, IT infrastructure, and technical education. He holds AS/AAS degrees in electrical troubleshooting from Yavapai College, and is currently pursuing a BS in electrical engineering at Arizona State University's Ira A. Fulton Schools of Engineering and working to complete the honors program at ASU's Barrett, The Honors College.

He brings a process-improvement mindset to every engagement — whether diagnosing a hardware failure, designing a tutoring plan, or streamlining a workflow. Detail-oriented by training and systems-minded by disposition, he is equally comfortable on the bench and in the classroom.

Available for contract engineering work, technical consulting, and private tutoring. Get in touch.
