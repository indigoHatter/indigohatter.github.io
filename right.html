Bio

Porter Blakeley is an Arizona-based actor and theatre technician with 10+ years of experience in community and regional theatre. He has appeared in productions including Waiting for Godot as Estragon, The Two Gentlemen of Verona as Valentine, and a devised original work titled It's A Wonderful Life? (yes, with a question mark) as Uncle Billy. He's also served as stage manager, lighting designer, sound operator, stagehand, and myriad other roles on dozens of productions across theatres & companies.

Equally at home in drama and comedy, Porter brings a technical precision to performance work — an instinct for detail and timing honed by years of running light boards and calling cues. He is a committed collaborator who believes the best theatre happens when every department trusts each other completely.
